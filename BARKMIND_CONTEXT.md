# BarkMind Context

## Identity

BarkMind is an open canine behavior intelligence platform focused on:
- canine body language
- behavior review
- escalation analysis
- structured behavioral annotation
- expert consensus
- AI-assisted interpretation

This is NOT:
- a social media pet app
- meme-focused
- generic pet content

This platform should feel:
- scientific
- operational
- community-driven
- AI-native
- credible to trainers, daycare leaders, groomers, shelter staff, and veterinary teams

## Long-Term Vision

BarkMind is intended to become:
- the world’s best canine behavioral intelligence dataset
- a multimodal canine behavior research platform
- an AI-assisted dog behavior copilot ecosystem

Future capabilities may include:
- stress detection
- escalation prediction
- daycare grouping recommendations
- veterinary restraint guidance
- shelter intake support
- behavioral risk analysis
- posture/frame analysis
- multimodal fine-tuning datasets

## MVP Focus

The MVP focuses ONLY on:
- behavior review cases
- image/video uploads
- structured tagging
- community comments
- expert resolutions
- AI-assisted summaries
- searchable case intelligence

## Aegis Alignment

BarkMind must:
- comply with AI_CONTEXT.md
- expose governance endpoints
- support future Aegis orchestration
- support future OpenClaw routing
- remain Docker-ready
- support future autonomous build sessions
- support OBS/live-stream-friendly development workflows

## Build Philosophy

Avoid:
- overengineering
- fake AI complexity
- premature agents
- bloated microservices

Prioritize:
- operational clarity
- clean architecture
- extensibility
- governance visibility
- production-minded systems
